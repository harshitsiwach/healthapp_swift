# Social & Privacy Rules

## Goal
Protect sensitive health data by ensuring the social layers and leaderboards run strictly on **opt-in** behavior, consistency, and gamified XP—not medical conditions or exact biometric values.

## Privacy Tiers
1. **Private (Default)**:
   - Everything visible only to the user.
   - Leaderboard defaults to offline/ghost mode (no name shown).
2. **Friends Only**:
   - Basic profile (Name, Rank, Level).
   - Streaks, Badges, Chosen routine.
3. **Public**:
   - Bio.
   - Badge showcase.
   - Public challenges participation.
   - Lifestyle templates (shared routines).

## Prohibited Data in Social
Never publish:
- Medical reports or Health tab summaries.
- Diagnosis summaries.
- Exact `HKQuantitySample` values (e.g., exact blood pressure, heart rate).
- Medication lists.
- Raw OCR health documents.
- Abnormal-lab outputs.

## Following Lifestyles
Users follow **routines**, not biometrics. 
A "Follow Lifestyle" action copies a public `RoutineTemplate` into the user's setup (e.g., wake time, hydration goal, meal cadence) to inspire healthy habits safely.
